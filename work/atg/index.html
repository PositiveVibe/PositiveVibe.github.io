Promo-tools #

One of my notable accomplishments was the development of Promo-tools, a dynamic ad template generation system that ran inside our affiliate portal. As the only developer of the project, I was responsible for seeing this product through to launch. New Mobile Join Form #

Another project that I worked on was the Join Application, which allowed A/B testing on the new member funnel for over 2000 white labels. I was responsible coding out the new mobile front-end.

The results of the project increased the CTR for the new join form by 10% and as a result was pushed across all white labels and has netted millions of dollars at this point.

General Creative Development and A/B Testing #

Furthermore, I created ad/funnel optimization roadmaps to deliver consistent growth against key performance metrics. I also defined and reported on KPI’s for new funnels and campaigns, and planned and implemented hundreds of user and A/B tests to optimize the conversion rate on landing pages, funnels, and pre-rolls.
